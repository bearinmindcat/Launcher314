# Privacy Policy

**Launcher314** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No internet connection is required or used
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
- No third-party data collection libraries
- All app data (settings, layout, folders) is stored locally on your device only
- No data is shared with the developer or any third party

This app requires the following permissions:
- **QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES** — To display installed apps in the app drawer (Android 11+ requirement)
- **REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES** — To allow app uninstall from the launcher
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** — For optional persistent notification quick access
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED** — To restore notification on device restart
- **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES** — To display your wallpaper

No data from these permissions is collected or transmitted.

If you have questions, open an issue at https://github.com/bearinmindcat/Launcher314/issues
